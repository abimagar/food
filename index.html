Our Story

Founded in 1985 by Giovanni Rossi, an Italian immigrant with a passion for authentic Neapolitan pizza, Slice Heaven has been serving the community for over 35 years.

Giovanni brought with him traditional recipes passed down through generations and a wood-fired oven built with bricks from Mount Vesuvius.

Today, under the leadership of Giovanni's son Marco, we continue the tradition of crafting pizzas the way they're meant to be - with care, quality ingredients, and a touch of love.

Marco Rossi, Head Chef
